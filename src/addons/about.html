Instant Valuation Estimates

Provide Instant Home Valuations with Ease

With the Plunk Home Valuation Widget, visitors to your website can quickly and easily get an estimate of their home's value. This provides a valuable service that can help you attract and engage potential clients, while also positioning yourself as a knowledgeable and trustworthy real estate professional.

Precise address search

Show Property's Value Instantly

Plunk's Home Valuation Widget offers an intuitive auto-complete feature that allows users to quickly input their property's address. By simply typing in the first few letters of their address, the tool will suggest a full address with Google's verified accuracy.

Easy lead capture

Accurate and Real-Time Home Valuations

Plunk's Home Valuation Widget provides a quick and reliable home value estimate based on real-time market data. Additionally, the widget offers a lead capture feature that enables users to receive an email with their estimated home value and market analysis.

Connect with leads

Even if No Results are Found, Plunk's Widget Helps You Get More Leads

If a user's address is not listed in the widget's database, the tool offers an option to input their phone number. Plunk's Home Valuation Widget also enables your users to connect with you to get further assistance. This ensures that potential leads never go unanswered.

Engage with insights

Market Insights with Plunk's Customizable Email Feature

When a lead requests market insights, Plunk's Widget sends an automated email with a detailed report with statistics that includes information on price variation, price per sqft, median days on market, competing listings, and more.

Keep track of lead requests

Manage And Track Leads Effectively

Plunk's Home Valuation Widget offers a user-friendly dashboard that allows agents and brokers to view a list of requests made by potential leads. The dashboard provides a summary of each request, including the property address and contact information.
